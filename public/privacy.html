Privacy Policy for Quranic App

Last Updated: July 5, 2025

Welcome to Quranic ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Quranic mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

The Quranic App is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide and improve our services:

  • App Usage Data: We may collect anonymous analytics about how you interact with the App to improve user experience.
  • Device Information: Basic device information (type, OS version) for compatibility purposes.
  • Audio Data: When you play Quran recitations, we temporarily cache audio files for smooth playback.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Improve user experience and app performance
  • Monitor usage patterns to enhance features
  • Troubleshoot technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All audio data is streamed and not permanently stored on your device
  • Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses these third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Analytics: For anonymous usage statistics
  • Firebase: For crash reporting and performance monitoring
  • Quranic API: To serve Quranic content and recitations

5. Permissions

The App requests these permissions:

  • Internet: To stream Quran recitations
  • Media Playback Control: To allow audio control from notifications/lockscreen

6. Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will take steps to remove the information.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and posting the new policy in the App.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@thequranic.site
